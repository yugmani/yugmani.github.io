Master Coder in Training: My Story
I am a web developer. I have good knowledge of front-end techniques. I started my career as a middle school teacher after my graduation in mathematics. When I moved into this new world (USA), I was not able to continue my career as a teacher due to different kinds of requirements and credentials. This moment was really hard to choose a new career among the limited options that I had.

I used to love working in computer and surfing internet. Hard working and deep learning mostly through the self-learning and innovation have always been my way of gaining knowledge and skills. Consequently, I got confidence to delve into the arena of web development. Intensive coding bootcamp training at UCLA Extension have been a dream institution for the most talent and innovative developers to become a highly skillful leader in the field. Fortunately, I got opportunity to rock into this platform.
